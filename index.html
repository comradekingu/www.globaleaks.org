Anonymous whistleblowing

Free speech where otherwise impossible, dangerous, or unplesant. Customizable to the needs of a private person or an organization.

Your information, your data

GlobaLeaks does not restrict or spy on users. Its workings are in the open to be studied The rights of the individual extends to the software, make it so by sharing and helping, by and for all to enjoy.

One piece of the puzzle

  • LeakDirectory gives publicity to initiatives. WikiLeaks handles documents. GlobaLeaks is an unaffiliated software framework in the public interest.

Transparency for all

When everyone is held accountable for their actions, there is no backlash. Instead it instills respect and trust as a basis of cooperation.

The free flow of information counters hidden dangers to democracy and economy. Protecting this basic right in the digital age keeps things honest and fair.
